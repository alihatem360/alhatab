OUR STORY

With a strong passion for baking, Al Hatab Bakery emerged to provide its customers with a wide variety of freshly baked goods and gourmet specialties. Our bakery produces a diverse line of fresh breads, decadent sweets and delicate pastries allowing customers to experience the art of baking from the guru hands of passionate and experienced chefs.
Al Hatab Bakery is present all over the kingdom providing an array of local and international bakery goods, as well as signature salads and appetizers from all around the world. Not only do we cater intercontinental cuisines, but we also reflect our Saudi roots in fine authentic national sweets, ranging from Klija to Maamoul, and any other item our customers would desire.
Inspired by earthy flavors and wholesome ingredients, Al Hatab Bakery takes pride in preparing authentic national and international sweets and breads and satisfying both sweet and salty cravings. Sink your teeth into delicious Arabian cuisines, experience Latin delicacies and indulge in European specialties, all that and much more. Our outlets are also famous for preparing distinguished Mediterranean and Asian recipes and fusions granting food lovers the opportunity to experience different flavors from all around the world.
